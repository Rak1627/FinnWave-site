ABOUT FINNWAVE

FinnWave superior quality basmati rice is characterized by its exquisite fragrance, delicate texture and is cultivated exclusively in Punjab’s rich fields and nurtured by Himalayan snow-fed rivers.

FinnWave basmati rice has a distinctive fine fragrance, long and slender kernels, soft texture and a taste of immaculate quality. It is ideal for all savoury dishes and makes every event exceptional.

Recipes Chinese Egg Fried Rice

Take a look at these recipes to prepare delicious meals with FinnWave Basmati rice and add flavour to your food.

Method:

  • 1. In a pan add water, rice and salt, place on stove for boiling, when cooked drain .
  • 2. Fry chopped onions in heated oil in a pan until they turn light brown in colour.
  • 3. Beat eggs in a bowl, add salt, black pepper, Chinese salt and whisk again.
  • 4. Pour beaten eggs to browned onions and mix.
  • 5. Once eggs are cooked, add chopped green onions and soy sauce.
