About FinnWave Basmati Rice

Basmati rice is called the “King of Rice” with an undisputed supremacy across the world. Basmati rice grows only in the foothills of the Himalayas, in the region of Punjab. FinnWave imports rice from Pakistan under the brand name FinnWave Basmati Rice.

FinnWave launched FinnWave Basmati Rice brand with a vision to make it the premier rice brand, preferred and consumed by most people in Finland. Consistent quality and satisfaction is guaranteed on every purchase, this promise is the core value of our business. Today FinnWave Basmati Rice is available everywhere in Finland and is preferred by food consumers.

FinnWave Basmati Rice is a cause to celebrate the flavours of life everyday with an aroma that goes beyond the borders of cultures and nations, enhancing the flavour of cuisines in every kitchen, restaurant and occasion.

We focus on consistent quality to delight our customers with international standards, quality and purity of rice. We make sure that our every action conveys nothing but excellence.
